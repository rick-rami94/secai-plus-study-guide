# SecAI+ Study Guide (CY0-001)

*Unofficial study material aligned to the official CompTIA SecAI+ CY0-001 V1 objectives — verify against the official objectives. See [`../exam-objectives.md`](../exam-objectives.md).*

Work through the domains in order; **spend the most time on Domain 2 (40% of the exam).** Keep the glossary and frameworks crosswalk open alongside.

| # | Chapter | Weight | Covers |
|---|---------|:------:|--------|
| 1.0 | [Basic AI Concepts Related to Cybersecurity](domain-1-ai-concepts.md) | 17% | AI types & training, prompt engineering, data security for AI, the AI life cycle, human-centric design |
| 2.0 | [**Securing AI Systems**](domain-2-securing-ai-systems.md) | **40%** | Threat-modeling resources, security/gateway/access/data controls, monitoring & auditing, attacks → compensating controls |
| 3.0 | [AI-assisted Security](domain-3-ai-assisted-security.md) | 24% | Using AI tools for security tasks, how AI enables attacks, automating security with AI |
| 4.0 | [AI Governance, Risk, and Compliance](domain-4-governance-risk-compliance.md) | 19% | Governance structures & AI roles, responsible AI & risks, compliance (EU AI Act, OECD, ISO, NIST AIRMF) |
