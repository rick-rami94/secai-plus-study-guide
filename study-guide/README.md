# SecAI+ Study Guide

*Unofficial / community study material. Verify against the official CompTIA objectives — see [`../exam-objectives.md`](../exam-objectives.md).*

Work through the domains in order; keep the glossary and frameworks crosswalk open alongside.

| # | Chapter | Weight | Covers |
|---|---------|:------:|--------|
| 1.0 | [AI & Security Foundations](domain-1-foundations.md) | 16% | AI/ML concepts, architectures, the AI threat landscape, core frameworks, shared responsibility |
| 2.0 | [AI Threats, Attacks & Vulnerabilities](domain-2-threats-attacks.md) | 24% | Adversarial ML, LLM/GenAI attacks, supply chain, agentic risks, data/privacy, OWASP/ATLAS mapping |
| 3.0 | [Securing the AI/ML Lifecycle](domain-3-securing-lifecycle.md) | 22% | Data, training, deployment/inference, identity, RAG security, guardrails, testing/red teaming |
| 4.0 | [AI Governance, Risk & Compliance](domain-4-governance-risk-compliance.md) | 18% | NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, EU AI Act, risk & impact assessment, ethics/fairness, policy/acceptable use |
| 5.0 | [AI Security Operations & Incident Response](domain-5-secops-incident-response.md) | 20% | Logging/monitoring, detection, AI-aware IR, resilience/recovery, vuln mgmt |
